THE DETAILS

N0RTH STAR

The North Star inclusion, sitting above the state abbreviation mimics the star’s position in the sky as our guiding light. When displayed, the star acts as a force – leading, uniting and inspiring people at every step on the journey to greatness.

c0nstellati0n

The collection of stars, running along the right side of the uniform, represent the connection amongst people in Minnesota and around the world.

STATE IC0N

The state icon represents us as the North Star State and commemorates all the individuals who are connected to this domain.

aur0ra green

The subtle but strong hints of green that consume the uniform are symbolic to the vibrant hue that shines by way of the Aurora Borealis.

midnight black

The uniform’s main hue reflects the mystique and dark characteristics of a Minnesota night – a time when the North Star and other surrounding stars are shining their brightest.
